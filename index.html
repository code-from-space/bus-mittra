About BUS Mittra

In India, public buses are one of the most widely used modes of transport. Despite this, there is no unified digital system that provides reliable information about bus routes, timings, live location, or crowd conditions. Passengers often wait without knowing when the bus will arrive or whether the bus is overcrowded.

BUS Mittra is developed to solve this problem by offering a smart, technology-driven solution for public transportation. The platform allows users to track buses in real time, view routes and stops, receive arrival notifications, and plan their journeys efficiently.

For bus drivers, BUS Mittra provides route guidance and emergency alert features. For government authorities, it offers a centralized dashboard to monitor bus operations, receive alerts, and improve service quality.

This project is designed as an academic prototype to demonstrate how digital systems can improve public transport infrastructure and enhance the overall commuter experience.

Passenger Complaint & Feedback

BUS Mittra provides passengers with a digital platform to raise complaints and share feedback related to bus services. This helps authorities identify issues such as delays, overcrowding, misbehavior, or safety concerns.
